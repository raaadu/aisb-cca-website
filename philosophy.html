Our Philosophy

CREATIVITY PHOTO: Art or drama activity
Pillar One

Creativity

At AISB, creative expression is at the heart of our CCA program. Through the arts, performance, music, and innovation challenges, students explore new ideas and ways of thinking. We believe every student is creative — our role is to give them the time, space, and encouragement to discover that potential.

When students create something — a painting, a performance, a project — they build confidence and a deeper understanding of the world around them. Creativity is not just a skill; it is a way of approaching every challenge life presents.

COURAGE PHOTO: Athletics competition or public speaking
Pillar Two

Courage

Growth happens at the edge of comfort. Our CCAs are designed to gently push students beyond what feels safe and familiar — whether that means stepping onto an athletic field for the first time, standing on stage, or taking on a leadership role they have never held before.

We celebrate effort, not just results. When students take a risk and see it through — win or lose — they develop the resilience and self-belief that carries them through every stage of life. Courage is a practice, and our programs provide the arena to practice it every day.

COMPASSION PHOTO: Community service or group collaboration
Pillar Three

Compassion

Co-curricular activities are not just about individual achievement — they are about belonging. When students work together on a team, collaborate on a performance, or serve their community, they develop a deeper capacity for empathy and genuine care for others.

Our programs create spaces where students learn to listen, to support, and to recognize the humanity in everyone around them. These relationships and values are the ones that last long after graduation. Once a Vampire, always a Vampire.

Our primary focus is on creating positive experiences, building meaningful memories, and cultivating lasting relationships.

— AISB CCA Department
