Joining the ELC

Early Learning Center

The Early Learning Center (ELC) is AISB's dedicated program for our youngest Vampires — children ages 3 to 6 in our kindergarten division. Designed specifically for early childhood, the ELC offers a nurturing, stimulating environment where curious minds begin their AISB journey.

Our ELC programs focus on creative play, early arts, movement, music, and social development. Every activity is thoughtfully designed to support the whole child: building confidence, fostering friendships, and igniting a lifelong love of learning — all in a safe, joyful setting.

Enrollment in the ELC is open to all AISB families with children in the eligible age range. We warmly invite parents to reach out to our ELC Coordinator to learn more about our programs and begin the enrollment journey for your little one.

Getting Started

Programs for Our Youngest Vampires

1

Browse ELC Programs

For Parents

Frequently Asked

The ELC serves children ages 3 to 6 in our Early Learning Center division. This includes our Nursery, Pre-K, and Kindergarten groups. Each age group has its own dedicated program that matches the developmental stage and learning needs of the children within it.

ELC activities are designed to nurture the whole child through play and exploration. Our programs include creative arts and crafts, early music and movement, imaginative play sessions, outdoor discovery, and structured social activities — all facilitated by qualified early childhood educators who understand the magic of this age.

To enroll your child, start by downloading the ELC Parent Handbook using the button below — it contains all program details, schedules, and enrollment forms. You are also welcome to contact our ELC Coordinator directly by email or phone. We recommend reaching out early in the school year, as spaces in some age groups fill quickly.

Welcome to the ELC

A Warm Start for Every Family

Our orientation sessions are designed to put both children and parents at ease before the first day of ELC. You will meet the teaching team, tour the learning environment, and ask any questions you may have — all in a relaxed and welcoming setting.

We believe that a strong home-school partnership begins from the very first moment. The ELC team is committed to keeping parents informed, involved, and confident throughout their child's early learning journey at AISB.
