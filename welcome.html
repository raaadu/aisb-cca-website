Welcome

Welcome to the AISB CCA Department! We are dedicated to providing every student with meaningful co-curricular experiences that complement their academic journey. From athletics to the arts, leadership to service learning, our programs are designed to help students discover new passions, develop essential life skills, and build lasting friendships.

We believe that what happens outside the classroom is just as important as what happens inside it. Co-curricular activities give students the space to take risks, make mistakes, and grow in ways that cannot be measured on a test — through teamwork, resilience, creativity, and compassion.

Whether you are a first-time participant or a returning Vampire, there is a place for you here. We can't wait to see what you will create, achieve, and discover this year.
