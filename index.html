Introduction

At AISB, we consider co-curricular activities (CCAs) to be a vital part of a comprehensive whole-child education. The AISB CCA department offers students opportunities to express creativity, demonstrate courage, and show compassion through activities such as playing on athletic teams, performing on stage, and taking on leadership roles. These experiences are seen as instrumental in fostering the development of new skills, contributing to personal growth, and fostering a sense of belonging within the school and the broader community. Our primary focus is on creating positive experiences, building meaningful memories, and cultivating lasting relationships.

What We Stand For

Creativity

Students express themselves through the arts, athletics, and innovation — discovering new ways to think, make, and contribute.

Courage

Students step beyond their comfort zones, embrace challenge, and grow through meaningful risk, perseverance, and resilience.

Compassion

Students build empathy, support one another, and learn to contribute meaningfully to the community and the world around them.
